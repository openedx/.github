# Contributing to the Open edX project

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Open edX project!

You can find our [latest contribution guidelines on the Open edX Documentation Site](https://docs.openedx.org/en/latest/developers/quickstarts/so_you_want_to_contribute.html).

Contributions must follow the [Open edX Project's AI Policy](https://github.com/openedx/.github/blob/master/AI_POLICY.md).
